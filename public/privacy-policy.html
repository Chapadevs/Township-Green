1. Introduction

Welcome to Top of the Green ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our cannabis consumption lounge located at 17 E. Scott Street, Riverside, NJ 08075, use our website, or participate in our services.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We are committed to compliance with all applicable cannabis regulations and privacy laws.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name and date of birth (required for age verification)

We automatically collect certain information when you use our services:

2.3 Cannabis Compliance Information

In compliance with New Jersey cannabis regulations, we collect:

  • Age verification records (21+ requirement)

  • Compliance documentation as required by state law

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Age verification and legal compliance
  • Processing reservations and bookings
  • Providing customer service and support
  • Sending booking confirmations and updates
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Ensuring safety and security of our facility
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Marketing communications (with your consent)

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, regulation, or legal process

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Secure data encryption and storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments and updates

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

To exercise these rights, please contact us at admin@topofgreen.com or (609) 325-1339.
